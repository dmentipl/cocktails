## Ingredients

- 60 ml bourbon
- 30 ml lemon juice
- 15 ml sugar syrup
- 4 dashes orange bitters
- 1/2 egg white (optional)
- _(garnish)_ cocktail cherry
- _(garnish)_ orange twist

## Instructions

Dry shake ingredients. Then shake with ice. Serve in a rocks glass over ice. Garnish.
