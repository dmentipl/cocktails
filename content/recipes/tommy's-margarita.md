## Ingredients

- 60 ml tequila
- 30 ml lime juice
- 15 ml agave syrup
- _(garnish)_ lime slice

## Instructions

1. Shake ingredients.
2. Serve in a rocks glass over ice.
3. Garnish with a lime slice.
