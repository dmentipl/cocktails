## Ingredients

- 20 ml rye whiskey
- 20 ml brandy
- 20 ml sweet vermouth
- 10 ml Bénédictine liqueur
- 2 dashes creole bitters
- 2 dashes aromatic bitters
- _(garnish)_ lemon twist

## Instructions

1. Stirred.
2. Serve in a cocktail glass.
3. Garnish with a lemon twist.
