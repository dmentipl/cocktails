## Ingredients

- 22.5 ml rye whiskey
- 22.5 ml brandy
- 22.5 ml sweet vermouth
- 10 ml Bénédictine liqueur
- 2 dashes creole bitters
- 2 dash aromatic bitters
- lemon twist

## Instructions

1. Stirred.
2. Serve in a rocks glass without ice.
3. Garnish with a lemon twist.
