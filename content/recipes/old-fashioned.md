## Ingredients

- 60 ml rye whisky or bourbon
- 5 ml sugar syrup
- 4 dashes aromatic bitters
- _(garnish)_ orange twist
- _(garnish)_ cocktail cherry

## Instructions

1. Stir ingredients.
2. Serve in a rocks glass over ice.
3. Garnish with a orange twist and cocktail cherry.
