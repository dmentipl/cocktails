## Ingredients

- 50 ml rye whisky
- 25 ml dry vermouth
- 10 ml grenadine
- 4 dashes orange bitters
- 15 ml lemon juice
- _(garnish)_ lemon twist

## Instructions

Shake ingredients with ice. Serve in cocktail glass. Garnish.
