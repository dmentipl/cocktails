## Ingredients

- 60 ml gin
- 30 ml dry vermouth
- 2 dashes orange bitters
- _(garnish)_ lemon twist
- _(garnish)_ olives

## Instructions

1. Make sure the drink and glass are *cold*.
2. Stir ingredients.
3. Serve in a cocktail glass.
4. Garnish with a lemon twist or olives.
