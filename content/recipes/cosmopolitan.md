## Ingredients

- 60 ml vodka (citron)
- 30 ml triple sec
- 30 ml lime juice
- 15 ml cranberry juice
- _(garnish)_ lemon twist

## Instructions

Shake ingredients with ice. Serve in a cocktail glass. Garnish.
