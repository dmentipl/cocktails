## Ingredients

- 60 ml prosecco
- 45 ml Aperol
- 20 ml soda water
- _(garnish)_ orange slice
- _(garnish)_ olive

## Instructions

1. Build in wine glass over ice.
2. Garnish with orange slice and olive.
