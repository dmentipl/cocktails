## Ingredients

- 60 ml rye whisky
- 5 ml sugar syrup
- 4 dashes creole bitters
- 4 dashes absinthe
- _(garnish)_ lemon twist

## Instructions

Rinse glass with absinthe. Stir ingredients over ice. Serve in a rocks glass without ice. Garnish.
