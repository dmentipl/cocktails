## Ingredients

- 30 ml brandy
- 30 ml rye whisky or bourbon
- 30 ml sweet vermouth
- 2 dashes aromatic bitters
- _(garnish)_ orange twist

## Instructions

Stir ingredients over ice. Serve in a cocktail glass. Garnish.
