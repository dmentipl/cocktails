Negroni

#campari #gin #sweet vermouth

Ingredients

  • 30 ml gin
  • 30 ml Campari
  • 30 ml sweet vermouth
  • 1/2 slice orange

Instructions

  1. Stir ingredients.
  2. Serve in rocks glass over ice.
  3. Garnish with orange slice.
