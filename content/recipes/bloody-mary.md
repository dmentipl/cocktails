## Ingredients

- 45 ml vodka
- 90 ml tomato juice
- 15 ml lemon juice
- 2 dashes Worcestershire sauce
- 2 dashes hot sauce
- pinch celery salt
- pinch pepper
- _(garnish)_ celery stalk
- _(garnish)_ lemon wedge

## Instructions

Build in highball glass over ice. Garnish.
