## Ingredients

- 60 ml rum
- 20 ml lime juice
- 20 ml sugar syrup
- a few mint leaves
- soda water
- _(garnish)_ mint sprig

## Instructions

Crush ice in clean tea towel. Lightly clap mint. Add ingredients except soda to highball glass. Add crushed ice and stir briefly, and top with soda water. Garnish.
