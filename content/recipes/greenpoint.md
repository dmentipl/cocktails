## Ingredients

- 60 ml rye whisky or bourbon
- 30 ml yellow Chartreuse
- 30 ml sweet vermouth
- 2 dashes aromatic bitters
- _(garnish)_ orange twist

## Instructions

1. Stir ingredients.
2. Serve in a cocktail glass.
3. Garnish with a orange twist.
