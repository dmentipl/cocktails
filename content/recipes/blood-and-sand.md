## Ingredients

- 20 ml Scotch
- 20 ml blood orange juice
- 20 ml sweet vermouth
- 20 ml cherry liqueur
- _(garnish)_ orange zest
- _(garnish)_ cocktail cherry

## Instructions

Shake ingredients with ice. Serve in a cocktail glass. Garnish.
