## Ingredients

- 30 ml Scotch
- 30 ml Oloroso sherry
- 15 ml lemon juice
- 15 ml grenadine or redcurrant or raspberry syrup
- _(garnish)_ lemon twist

## Instructions

Shake ingredients with ice. Serve in cocktail glass. Garnish.
