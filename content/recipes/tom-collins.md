## Ingredients

- 60 ml gin
- 30 ml lemon juice
- 15 ml sugar syrup
- soda water
- _(garnish)_ lemon or lime slice

## Instructions

Add ingredients except soda to highball glass and stir to combine. Add soda water to halfway up glass. Fill glass with ice. Garnish.
