## Ingredients

- 60 ml rye whisky or bourbon
- 30 ml amaro
- 2 dashes aromatic bitters
- 2 dashes orange bitters
- cherry

## Instructions

1. Stir ingredients.
2. Serve in a cocktail glass.
3. Garnish with a cherry.
