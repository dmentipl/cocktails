## Ingredients

- 60 ml tequila
- 30 ml lime juice
- 15 ml triple sec
- _(garnish)_ lime slice

## Instructions

Rim cocktail glass with salt. Shake ingredients with ice. Serve in cocktail glass. Garnish.
