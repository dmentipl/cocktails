## Ingredients

- 60 ml rye whisky or bourbon
- 5 ml sugar syrup
- 4 dashes aromatic bitters
- _(garnish)_ orange twist
- _(garnish)_ cocktail cherry

## Instructions

Stir ingredients over ice. Serve in a rocks glass over ice. Garnish.
