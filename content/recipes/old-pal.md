## Ingredients

- 30 ml rye whisky
- 30 ml Campari
- 30 ml dry vermouth
- _(garnish)_ orange slice

## Instructions

1. Stir ingredients.
2. Serve in rocks glass over ice.
3. Garnish with orange slice.
