## Ingredients

- 45 ml gin
- 45 ml sweet vermouth
- 5 ml Maraschino liqueur
- 4 dashes aromatic bitters
- _(garnish)_ lemon twist

## Instructions

1. Stirred over ice.
2. Serve in a cocktail glass.
3. Garnish with lemon twist.
