## Ingredients

- 45 ml pisco
- 30 ml lime juice
- 15 ml sugar syrup
- 1/2 egg white
- _(garnish)_ aromatic bitters
- _(garnish)_ lime slice

## Instructions

Dry shake ingredients. Then shake with ice. Serve in a rocks glass without ice. Garnish with drops of bitters and a lime slice.
