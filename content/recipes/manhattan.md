## Ingredients

- 60 ml rye whisky or bourbon
- 20 ml sweet vermouth
- 4 dashes aromatic bitters
- _(garnish)_ cocktail cherry

## Instructions

Stir ingredients over ice. Serve in a cocktail glass. Garnish.
