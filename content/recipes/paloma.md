_Note: you can make the drink fancy by substituting the grapefruit soda for grapefruit juice, agave syrup, and soda water._

## Ingredients

- 60 ml tequila
- 120 ml grapefruit soda
- 5 ml lime juice
- pinch of salt
- _(garnish)_ lime wedge

## Instructions

Mix together in a highball glass over ice. Garnish.
