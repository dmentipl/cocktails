## Ingredients

- 45 ml bourbon or rye whisky
- 15 ml Campari
- 15 ml sweet vermouth
- _(garnish)_ orange twist

## Instructions

1. Stir ingredients.
2. Serve in a cocktail glass.
3. Garnish with orange twist.
