Amaro Nonino is expensive so feel free to substitute for a similar (or different) amaro.

## Ingredients

- 20ml bourbon
- 20ml Aperol
- 20ml Amaro Nonino
- 20ml lemon juice
- _(garnish)_ lemon twist or miniature paper plane

## Instructions

Shake ingredients with ice. Serve in a cocktail glass. Garnish.
