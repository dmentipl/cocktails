## Ingredients

- 60 ml rye whiskey
- 22.5 ml sweet vermouth
- 22.5 ml Bénédictine liqueur
- 3 dashes absinthe
- 3 dashes creole bitters
- _(garnish)_ cocktail cherry

## Instructions

1. Stirred.
2. Serve in a cocktail glass.
3. Garnish with a cocktail cherry.
