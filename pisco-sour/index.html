Pisco sour

#bitters #egg-white #lime-juice #pisco #sugar-syrup

Ingredients

  • 45 ml pisco
  • 30 ml lime juice
  • 15 ml sugar syrup
  • 1/2 egg white
  • (garnish) aromatic bitters
  • (garnish) lime slice

Instructions

Dry shake ingredients. Then shake with ice. Serve in a rocks glass without ice. Garnish with drops of bitters and a lime slice.
