## Ingredients

- 60 ml gin
- 30 ml lime juice
- 15 ml sugar syrup
- 1 mint sprig
- _(garnish)_ 1 mint leaf

## Instructions

Shake ingredients with ice. Serve in a cocktail glass. Garnish.
