## Ingredients

- 20 ml gin
- 20 ml triple sec
- 20 ml Lillet Blanc
- 20 ml lemon juice
- 4 dashes absinthe (optional)
- _(garnish)_ cocktail cherry

## Instructions

Rinse glass with absinthe. Shake ingredients with ice. Serve in a cocktail glass. Garnish.
