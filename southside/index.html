Need a drink?

Southside

#gin #lime juice #mint #sugar syrup

Ingredients

  • 60 ml gin
  • 30 ml lime juice
  • 15 ml sugar syrup
  • 1 mint sprig
  • (garnish) 1 mint leaf

Instructions

  1. Shaken (without mint).
  2. Rolled (with mint).
  3. Serve in a cocktail glass.
  4. Garnish with mint leaf.
